07 — Next quarter

What we do with this.

Six moves that follow directly from the numbers above — designed to protect what worked and fix what didn't.

1

Re-sign the two hero creators

kelly.rebelwhitecoat and filmsandfitness drove 73% of GMV. Lock repeat content with both and secure them before competitors do — the single highest-leverage action for Q3.

2

Build a reliable mid-tier

Leaning on two creators is a risk. Recruit and activate 5–10 consistent performers so a single drop-off can't halve the program's output.

3

Lead every brief with Colon 14-Day

It's the proven converter, carrying the clear majority of GMV. Make it the default hook for new creators; introduce secondary SKUs only once they're landing sales.

4

Invest in Spanish creators

una_flor_cubana produced $698 from a single video — the segment is high-upside but thin at just 15 converting videos. A focused Spanish recruitment push is low-cost, high-ceiling.

5

Diagnose the June drop

Confirm whether June's dip is attribution lag or a genuine fall-off. If it's real, restoring monthly momentum becomes the quarter's headline objective.

6

Reconcile to Seller Center

These figures are video-attributed. Pull the full Creators view to fold in LIVE and product-card sales and confirm the totals as official numbers of record.
